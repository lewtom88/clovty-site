About Clovty

An institutional approach to commerce intelligence.

Clovty was founded on the principle that decision-grade commerce intelligence requires the combination of machine-scale data analysis with senior analytical judgment, delivered in writing, with full source attribution. The firm is built around that principle.

Background

Established for the teams underwriting category decisions.

Clovty was established in 2026 in Sheridan, Wyoming, to provide e-commerce intelligence services to enterprise commerce, brand, and investment teams. The firm was founded on the observation that the e-commerce-intelligence market had become saturated with self-service software platforms, while demand for senior-led analytical advisory remained underserved.

Clovty operates as an analytical-advisory firm rather than a software vendor. Engagements are fixed in scope, defined in writing, and delivered with full source attribution. Machine-learning analysis provides the quantitative foundation; senior analysts provide the synthesis, the written conclusion, and the named accountability.

Clovty engages with consumer brands, online and omnichannel retailers, marketplace operators, and commerce-focused investors. Engagements are accepted from clients in jurisdictions where the firm can lawfully provide services. Each engagement is sized to deliver a single decision-grade conclusion within a defined timeline, supported by audit-ready evidence.

Operating principles

Six operating principles.

Principle 01

Source attribution required.

Every claim in a Clovty deliverable carries an attribution. Findings without verifiable sources are not included. Source confidence and methodological limitations are stated explicitly.

Principle 02

Public and licensed data only.

Clovty operates exclusively on publicly available data and licensed third-party datasets. The firm does not scrape authenticated content, acquire leaked datasets, or process data obtained in violation of platform terms of service.

Principle 03

Quantitative scale, analyst-led synthesis.

Machine-learning analysis provides the quantitative foundation. The engagement lead performs synthesis and authorship. Every deliverable carries a named lead and is supported by a debrief session.

Principle 04

Engagement fit verified before scope.

Engagements are accepted only where Clovty has the data, methodology, and senior bandwidth to deliver against the stated question within the proposed timeline. Misaligned mandates are declined or referred at the briefing stage.

Principle 05

Negative recommendations are valid outputs.

A meaningful share of Clovty deliverables conclude against the action a client was evaluating. The firm considers a well-supported negative recommendation a valuable engagement outcome.

Principle 06

Compliant U.S. operations.

Clovty is a U.S.-registered limited liability company with a federal tax identification number assigned by the U.S. Internal Revenue Service. All invoicing and engagements are conducted in U.S. dollars. The firm maintains records in accordance with applicable U.S. accounting and tax requirements and does not process informal payments.
