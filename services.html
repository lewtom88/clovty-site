Solutions

Four enterprise practices, each delivered with technical rigor.

Clovty engagements are structured as fixed-scope projects or strategic retainers. Each deliverable is senior-led and supported by auditable data modules, ensuring compliance with enterprise information standards.

Market & Category Intelligence

Strategic demand mapping for brand and category leaders evaluating market entry or regional expansion.

Core deliverables

  • → Regional demand sizing and competitive density indexing across major commerce ecosystems
  • → Pricing architecture and margin-band analysis for category benchmarking
  • → Trajectory modeling: 24-month forecasts on search interest and consumer signals
  • → Executive brief containing decision-grade conclusions and source register

Typical timeline: 3–4 weeks · PDF Brief & Analytical Data Module

Custom AI Analysis

Bespoke software and analytical pipelines designed for merchandising and analytics teams with proprietary research requirements.

Project scope

  • → Deployment of custom NLP pipelines for large-scale consumer sentiment classification
  • → Machine-learning models for price-elasticity studies and assortment rationalization
  • → Automated data normalization across disparate multi-region commerce datasets
  • → Technical documentation and code-base delivery for client-side integration

Typical timeline: 4–8 weeks · Technical Report & Code Modules

Trend & Demand Monitoring

Continuous intelligence programs for category managers requiring early-inflection detection and competitive signal monitoring.

Advisory components

  • → Monthly analytical briefs covering watched category shifts and SKU-level movements
  • → Anomaly-detection alerts for pricing shifts or significant competitive entrants
  • → Quarterly strategy recalibration sessions with the lead data architect

Retainer Model: USD Retainer · 6-month minimum term

Commercial Due Diligence

For institutional investors and M&A teams conducting technical validation of digital-commerce assets.

Diligence framework

  • → Independent validation of category revenue claims through quantitative modeling
  • → Defensibility assessment: Listing health, review integrity, and sentiment profile
  • → Risk-matrix report highlighting category saturation and structural vulnerabilities

Timeline: 2–4 weeks (High-priority) · Confirmed NDA Required

Service Guardrails

Engagement boundaries.

Clovty operates exclusively as a commerce intelligence and research firm. The following activities are explicitly excluded from our scope of services:

  • — Product sourcing, manufacturing, shipping, or fulfillment.
  • — Advertising management (PPC/Media buying) or marketplace account operation.
  • — Legal, tax, or regulatory advisory (clients are referred to qualified counsel).
  • — Management of authenticated platform credentials or private customer data.
  • — Acceptance of informal value-transfer payments or cryptocurrencies.
